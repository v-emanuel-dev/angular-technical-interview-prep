Preparing for Interviews

Prepare examples of your work: Have examples of your projects Angled anteriors ready to show the interviewer.

Be prepared to talk about your experience with Angular, what you built and the challenges you faced and overcame.

Learn the fundamentals of software development: Improve the fundamentals of software development, such as algorithms, frameworks data and design patterns. Learn the fundamentals of development web: enhance HTML, CSS and JavaScript as these are technologies essential for Angular development.

Search the company: Search the Company and its projects to have a idea of what they do and what kind of projects they work on.

Practice troubleshooting technical issues: Practice troubleshooting technical issues related to Angular, such as writing code for implement a feature, debug an issue, or optimize performance.

Prepare questions for the interviewer: Prepare a list of questions What would you like to ask the interviewer to better understand the role and company.
